What is CS OutCrowd?

CS OutCrowd is a Twitch channel dedicated to Counter-Strike: Global Offensive.  Managed and played by "Lumi", the channel is dedicated to providing professional CS experience to all viewers and players alike, no matter what skill level.


CS OutCrowd is all about bringing a strong, positive attitude to the CS Community, while expanding its player base and collective skill level.  So check out the stream LIVE or catch up on the action you've missed, and get back into CS:GO.

Watch CS OutCrowd's "Lumi" in Action

CS OutCrowd's #1 is David"Luminaire" Helmken, a long time Counter-Strike veteran, who has a long history in the competetive scene of CS. With an entertaining twist on a serious game, Lumi is not only a beast in game, he brings a fully charged, positive attitude to the community.
